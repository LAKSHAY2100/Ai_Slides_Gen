FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does SlideAI create different styles of presentations?

SlideAI uses your selected theme and topic context to generate structure, tone, and visuals that match the style you choose.

Will I get the same output every time?

No. Each generation can produce a fresh variation so you can pick the version that best fits your presentation goal.

Can SlideAI avoid repeating mistakes?

Yes. When corrections are provided, SlideAI adapts and aims to avoid repeating the same mistakes in future outputs.

How many themes are available?

You can choose from 5 themes, allowing you to tailor the look and tone of your deck for different audiences.

Is SlideAI cost efficient for regular use?

Yes. It reduces manual work and speeds up deck creation, making presentation production faster and more cost efficient.
